About

I am Chua, a PhD student in Mathematics at the Universiti Malaya. My research interest lies in Abstract Algebra and Linear Algebra, through which my supervisor has guided me to explore the research fields of Functional Identities and Linear Preserver Problems.

Research interests: Strong commutativity preserving maps, Lie and Jordan structures, noncommutative rings, nonassociative algebras
